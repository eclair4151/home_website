Everything in one place

Built for how you actually ride

From the morning bus to the last train home, Route Radar gives you a live, accurate picture of the whole system.

Live vehicle map

Watch every active bus, trolley, subway, and regional rail train move in real time on a smooth, Metal-rendered map.

Real-time arrivals

Tap any stop for live, accurate ETAs that blend the schedule with real vehicle positions and delays.

Pinned stops

Save your everyday stops for instant arrivals, kept in sync across your devices with iCloud.

World-class navigation

Door-to-door directions across the whole network, powered by the same live data on the map — so the times you see are the times you'll ride.

Service alerts & detours

See active detours drawn right on the map and route alerts surfaced exactly where they matter — no digging.

Indego bikes & bike lanes

Find nearby Indego docks with live bike and dock availability — plus the city's bike-lane network mapped right alongside transit.

SEPTA Key, right in the app

Sign in to check your card balance, travel wallet, passes, and recent trips — without leaving Route Radar.
